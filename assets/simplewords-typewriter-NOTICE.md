# SimpleWords typewriter sound provenance

The five `simplewords-typewriter*.wav` files are the “Old typewriter” scheme
from the user-supplied WriteMonkey 2.7 portable archive:

- `key.wav` → `simplewords-typewriter.wav`
- `key2.wav` → `simplewords-typewriter-alt.wav`
- `space.wav` → `simplewords-typewriter-space.wav`
- `enter.wav` → `simplewords-typewriter-enter.wav`
- `delete.wav` → `simplewords-typewriter-delete.wav`

The WriteMonkey archive describes WriteMonkey as free software, but no
asset-specific redistribution license was present in the archive or on the
official download pages when these files were added. The SimpleSuite GPL does
not relicense these WAV files. Confirm redistribution permission with the
WriteMonkey author before distributing them outside a personal build.
